71% Blue



Home







Have you ever thought of what Earth will be like without the ocean? The ocean covers about 71% percent of the surface of the Earth and provides half of our oxygen. It absorbs carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and reduces the impact of climate change. It is our life-supporting system, and home to more than 700,000 species of animals.

Without the ocean, life could have never occured on Earth. It contains over 99% of the living space on Earth! Right now, the ocean is in danger. The Earth will no longer be 71% blue without our help. This website is going to tell you exactly what is happening to the ocean and how you can help.
