Other Websites
Here are some of the websites that we found that also talks about saving the oceans. They're really good for kids and adults alike and provides some great information about saving the ocean!


Website 1:
http://www.pegasusfoundation.org/marine-life-species-endangered-of-extinction/

This website talks about the different issues of endangered species face and why these animals are going extinct. With it’s clear cut organization, this website is easy to understand and makes you want to help the animals.



Website 2:
http://www.takepart.com/article/2016/09/14/large-marine-animals-are-going-be-hit-hardest-mass-extinction

This website talks about how we are soon to face a 6th extinction if we don’t help thae animals and what the 6th extinction means for us humans.



Website 3:
http://wwf.panda.org/what_we_do/how_we_work/our_global_goals/oceans/ocean_risk/

This is a website that looks targeted towards kids with it’s fun colors and fonts. It’s great for finding out what is causing the collapse of the ocean but not exactly for how to solve these problems.
Website 4:
https://www.theoceancleanup.com/

This I think, is my favorite website because not only does the website talk about how the ocean is, it also talks about the different ways you can help. My favorite part of the website however, is that they update you on ocean cleanups and technology.
